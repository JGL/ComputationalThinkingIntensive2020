1. Augmented Reality

  • WebXR viewer from Mozilla.
  • AR.js multiple marker demo. Print markers here.
  • A-Frame is a library for doing VR or AR on the web from Mozilla.
  • See this course that I did on the CSM MANE course last year for more information and background.

2. Computer Vision

  • See this course - "The Parallax View" that I did in September 2018 at Royal College of Art for lots of examples of computer vision, tracking and body tracking on the web.

3. Projection Mapping

  • The original Pablo Valbuena augmented sculpture project.
  • LASER tag by Graffiti Research Lab (RIP).
  • Night Lights by YesYesNo and Hellicar&Lewis.
  • Take a look at openFrameworks for making projection mapping projects. Check out ofxaddons.com too.
